About Mokutu HomeTech

We bring smart home technology to South African households with professional installation and dedicated support.

Who We Are

Mokutu HomeTech is a leading smart home technology company based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We specialize in providing modern smart-home upgrades and professional technology installations for homeowners who want to embrace the future of connected living.

Our team of certified technicians brings years of experience in home automation, networking, and security systems. We partner with top technology brands to deliver reliable solutions that enhance comfort, security, and energy efficiency in your home.

Our Mission

Making smart home technology simple, accessible, and reliable for every South African household.

We believe that everyone deserves to experience the convenience and security that modern technology brings. Our mission is to remove the complexity from smart home adoption by providing expert guidance, professional installation, and ongoing support.

From initial consultation to final setup and beyond, we're committed to ensuring your smart home works perfectly for your lifestyle and needs.

Our Expertise

We specialize in three core areas of smart home technology:

Smart Devices: From voice assistants and smart speakers to connected appliances and automated systems, we help you choose and configure the right devices for your home.

Home Networking: A reliable network is the foundation of any smart home. We design and install robust WiFi solutions that ensure seamless connectivity throughout your property.

Security Solutions: Protect what matters most with advanced security cameras, smart doorbells, and integrated alarm systems that you can monitor from anywhere.
